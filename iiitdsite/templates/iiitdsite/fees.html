Procedure

  • All New/Existing B. Tech. students should pay fees before registration date using State Bank Collect online portal.
  • Select Internet Banking or SBI debit card while paying fee through SBI Collect for lesser charges.
  • Students are advised to retain their receipt generated through State Bank Collect for proof of fee deposited.

Other Mode of Payment

  • Payment through Internet Banking : Payment can be done through NEFT/IMPS/UPI. For account details or any query regarding payment, you may write or call to contact@iiitdwd.ac.in, 9449732959
  • Payment through Credit Card: You can also make payment through Credit Card.
  • Cash is NOT accepted.
