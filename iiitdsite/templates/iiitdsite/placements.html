Career Guidance Cell

The Career Guidance Cell at IIIT Dharwad is driven by the students and assisted by the students for career development of students.
The goals of CGC are-

  • To provide placement drives at IIIT
  • To provide training and assistance to students for better placement and internship opportunities
  • To hone soft skills and interview techniques
  • To organize workshops and invited talks to make students industry ready
  • To assist students with their plans for higher studies
  • To provide information about entrance exams and other opportunities for higher studies
